Overview

Overwatch is a massive online battle arena (MOBA) type game that pits players to fight amongst one another in a solo or team type setting. The standalone version of the game regularly costs $49.99 and the game of the year edition is $74.99. Overwatch features many different game modes such as escort the payload, capture and hold the points.

Rules

The objective is the game is to work alone (Free-For-All) or with your team to complete the mission. Depending on the game-mode you are playing, the mission might be to escort the payload from one side of the map to the other, hold and capture a specific point, or simply kill the enemy team. The rules are pretty intuitive and will not take long to identify them. Overwatch also features an arcade mode, which allows access to even more game options.

History

Overwatch was created and released by Blizzard Entertainment on May 24, 2016.
